How to Play

It's easy enough for anyone to pick up, but you'll actually need to think about your moves later on.

Core Mechanics:

  • Match 3 or more identical tiles

  • Complete level objectives

  • Use boosters for difficult sections

  • Earn stars and rewards

  • Unlock and decorate new valley zones

Later levels introduce:

As you advance, levels become more challenging with blocked tiles, layered obstacles, and limited moves. You’ll need to think ahead, plan each move carefully, and use combos to trigger powerful bonus rewards and overcome tougher puzzles.
