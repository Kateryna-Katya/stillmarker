Match Valley

Match, Restore, and Bring the Valley Back to Life.

Solve puzzles, fix up the valley - it's pretty satisfying watching everything come together.

About the Game

Beat levels, get materials, then use those to spruce up farms, houses, gardens - you know, the works. We want the visuals to really show how the valley comes back to life as you play.

  • Classic match-3 mechanics

  • Level-based progression

  • Environmental restoration system

  • Boosters and combo explosions
